About

Kiran Haithcox has been teaching private Pilates at the The Working Body in Oakland for over 8 years. After graduating from Wesleyan University with a degree in Earth and Environmental Sciences and immersing herself in the world of modern dance in the Bay Area, Kiran trained to be a Pilates instructor with Nora St. John and Naomi of Turning Point Studios. Kiran’s love of learning, movement, and kinesthesiology combined with her desire to help people lead pain free lives fuels Kiran’s passion for teaching Pilates.
